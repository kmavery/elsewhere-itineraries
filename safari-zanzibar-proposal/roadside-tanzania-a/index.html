Route

Premium Tanzania circuit

The same classic Tanzania safari shape as Roadside Tanzania: B, but with higher-profile lodge names in Arusha, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro.

  1. Arrive Kilimanjaro/Arusha and overnight at Gran Melia Arusha.
  2. Fly to Serengeti for two nights at Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti.
  3. Drive/game-drive through Serengeti toward Melia Ngorongoro.
  4. Crater game drive, then fly onward to Zanzibar.
  5. Stone Town/Prison Island/snorkeling, then The Mora beach stay.
Flight segment Drive / game-drive Major stop
