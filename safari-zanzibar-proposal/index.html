Our recommendation

Lead with Roadside Tanzania: B

For a classic Tanzania honeymoon, Roadside Tanzania: B is the strongest value: 3 safari nights, 4 beach nights, private vehicle touring, Serengeti, Ngorongoro, and Zanzibar at a lower price than the upgraded lodge version.

andBeyond is the stronger safari product if Kenya is on the table. It is the only option with night drives, guided walks, private concession-style access, and a more exclusive camp experience.

The simple choice

Choose Roadside Tanzania: B for Tanzania highlights and value. Choose andBeyond for the richest safari experience. Choose Roadside Tanzania: A only if the upgraded lodge names are worth the extra spend.
