Our recommendation

Lead with Roadside Tanzania: B

If your priority is a classic Tanzania honeymoon - Serengeti, Ngorongoro, and Zanzibar - Roadside Tanzania: B is the strongest value. It keeps the same essential wildlife route as Roadside Tanzania: A while avoiding the full premium for the highest-end lodge names.

If you are open to Kenya and want the deepest safari experience, andBeyond is the most compelling alternative. It trades Serengeti/Ngorongoro for the Masai Mara, but adds night drives, guided walks, Kichwa Tembo, included flights, and very strong service polish.

The simple choice

Choose Roadside Tanzania: B for the best balance of Tanzania highlights, comfort, and price. Choose andBeyond if the safari experience itself matters most and Kenya is on the table.
