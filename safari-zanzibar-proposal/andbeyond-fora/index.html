Route

Kenya safari plus Zanzibar

A Kenya Masai Mara safari plus Zanzibar option with the strongest guiding/activity profile: Kichwa Tembo, night game drives, guided walks, included flights, and polished logistics.

  1. Arrive Nairobi and overnight at Villa Rosa Kempinski.
  2. Fly Wilson Airport to Kichwa Tembo Airstrip.
  3. Three nights at andBeyond Kichwa Tembo in the Masai Mara.
  4. Fly Kichwa Tembo to Wilson, then onward to Zanzibar.
  5. Stay at Melia Zanzibar or upgrade to Zuri Zanzibar.
Flight segment Drive / game-drive Major stop
